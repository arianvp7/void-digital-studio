FAQ

Quick answers.

How long does a project take?
Most web projects take 4–6 weeks from kick-off to launch. Branding projects run 2–3 weeks. Complex builds with custom functionality take 8–12 weeks.
Do you work with startups?
Absolutely. Some of our best work is with pre-revenue founders who needed a presence that punched above their weight. Budget-conscious packages available.
What's included in a website build?
Design, build, responsive layout, SEO optimisation, Vercel deployment, and 30 days of post-launch support. No hidden extras.
Do you offer ongoing retainers?
Yes. Monthly retainers are available for brands that want consistent design support — new pages, campaigns, ad creative, and iteration.
Can I see more work samples?
Yes — check the Work page for our full portfolio. For case studies specific to your industry, mention it in your message and we'll send relevant examples.
What do you need from me to start?
A rough brief, some reference sites you like, and your goals. We'll handle the rest — we're used to working from a napkin sketch.
